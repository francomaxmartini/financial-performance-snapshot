## Objective
The objective of this analysis was to evaluate the **financial performance** of a transactional business using a structured analytics approach. The project focused on assessing revenue, costs, profitability, and time-based trends while validating data quality and defining clear financial KPIs.

This analysis is intended to provide a **baseline financial snapshot** and demonstrate a repeatable framework suitable for ongoing monitoring and decision support.

---

## Dataset Overview
- Transaction-level financial data covering a **three-month period**.
- Complete time coverage: every day within the period contains at least one transaction.
- Minor data quality issues were identified (e.g., missing revenue values) and addressed through explicit cleaning rules.
- After validation, the dataset was considered reliable for KPI computation and trend analysis.

---

## Key Financial Results
- **Total Revenue:** 143,701  
- **Total Cost:** 88,873  
- **Gross Profit:** 54,829  
- **Gross Margin:** 38.15%  
- **Average Order Value:** 295.68  
- **Cost-to-Revenue Ratio:** 0.62  

Overall results indicate a **profitable operation** with a controlled cost structure and stable margins.

---

## Time-Based Performance Insights

### Daily Performance
- Daily revenue exhibits natural operational variability, ranging from approximately **48 to 4,112**, with an average of **~1,579**.
- Gross margin averages **~38%** at the daily level, with short-term fluctuations driven by transaction mix and volume.
- No extreme anomalies were detected that would materially distort KPI interpretation.

### Monthly Performance
- Revenue, profit, and margins remain relatively stable across the three months analyzed.
- A **mild decline in revenue and profit** is observed in the final month, which should be monitored but does not indicate a structural issue based on current data.

---

## Key Takeaways
- The business operates with **healthy profitability and sustainable margins**.
- Short-term volatility is present at the daily level, while monthly aggregation smooths most fluctuations.
- The defined KPIs provide a clear and interpretable view of financial performance.
- The analytical framework established here can be reused for:
  - Periodic performance reporting
  - Trend monitoring
  - Scenario and forecasting analyses
